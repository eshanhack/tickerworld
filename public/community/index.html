Community rules

Keep the little world kind.

Tickerworld is a shared public space. These rules apply to chat, names, emotes, reports, and any attempt to interact with other players.

Do

  • Be welcoming, especially to first-time players.
  • Talk about the world and markets without presenting financial advice as fact.
  • Use block and report tools when needed.
  • Respect that other players may be young, anonymous, or simply passing through.

Do not

  • Harass, threaten, shame, target, or repeatedly follow another player.
  • Post hate, slurs, explicit abuse, or sexual content.
  • Spam, flood, repeat messages, or coordinate artificial activity.
  • Share links, wallet or contract addresses, seed-phrase requests, phishing instructions, or “support” recovery offers.
  • Impersonate Tickerworld staff, support, administrators, public figures, or another player.
  • Evade a block, mute, kick, temporary ban, room limit, or other safety control.
  • Submit false reports to intimidate someone.

What happens

Automated filters may reject a message before it is shown. Players can block immediately. Reports include server-held recent room context rather than evidence edited by the reporter. Moderators may warn, mute, kick, throttle, or temporarily ban access, depending on severity and repetition.

Report urgent risk

Tickerworld is not an emergency service. If someone appears to be in immediate danger, contact the appropriate local emergency service. For product safety issues, use support and avoid posting private evidence publicly.
